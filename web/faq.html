Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why can't I book a reservation a day before?

    We only accept reservations that are 3 days in advance.

  • Can I reschedule my reservation 1 day before it?

    Yes, but if you request a reschedule with less than 3 business days before your reservation there is a Php 1250.00 fee.

  • Do I have to pay a down-payment if I am booking a tour?

    Yes, we require a 50% non-refundable down-payment upon reservation.

  • Can I cancel my reservation?

    You can cancel your reservation anytime. If your reservation has a group of 15-30 people, there is a Php 5000.00 no-show fee for groups that simply don't show up on the day of their tour without cancelling in advance.
